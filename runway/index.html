Built for Norway

Built for how money works in Norway

Most retirement calculators assume American rules: 401(k)s, Roth accounts, Social Security. A Norwegian gets a motivating number, not a real plan. Runway is built the other way around, on the rules that actually move your date here.

It models formuesskatt (wealth tax), the ASK account's capital-first withdrawals, the skjermingsfradrag, NAV and IPS pensions, and the utflyttingsskatt you would owe if you ever left Norway. For now Runway is made for Norway, and it is fully in Norwegian. More countries are coming next; until then, everywhere else runs on a solid generic baseline.
