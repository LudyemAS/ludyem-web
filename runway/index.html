Features

As simple as your life, as deep as a spreadsheet

Most planners are either shallow toys or overwhelming web apps built for US tax law. Runway is right-sized depth that fits in your pocket.

Your freedom age

The one number that matters: the age your money could carry you. It's the hook, and it's free forever, no account required.

Freedom number

The pot you actually need, derived from your real spending and a safe 4% withdrawal, net of pensions, rental, and side income.

The levers

An opportunity engine that finds the doors you didn't open: "invest 300 more a month, retire two years sooner." Every lever shows its effect.

Plan health

A seeded Monte Carlo runs your plan through 400 simulated market futures and scores how often your money lasts the distance.

Money over time

Watch your pot grow, peak, and draw down year by year, with your freedom goal line anchored to today so progress reads honestly.

Adaptive onboarding

No side income? Runway never mentions it. No property? It skips the mortgage questions. It asks only what fits your life, nothing more.

Scenario lab

Try the what-ifs side by side: a market crash, a later start, a different spend, a change in how you invest. Compare paths before you commit.

Built for Norway

Life events, real spending

Widgets and Siri

Keep your freedom date on your home and lock screen, ask Siri, or find your milestones in Spotlight. A glance is all it takes to see how close you are.

Built for Norway

Built for how money works in Norway

Most retirement calculators assume American rules: 401(k)s, Roth accounts, Social Security. A Norwegian gets a motivating number, not a real plan. Runway is built the other way around, on the rules that actually move your date here.

It models formuesskatt (wealth tax), the ASK account's capital-first withdrawals, the skjermingsfradrag, NAV and IPS pensions, and the utflyttingsskatt you would owe if you ever left Norway. And if leaving is the plan, Runway prices that too: pick one of 14 destination countries and see the whole move, from local drawdown taxes to the exit tax on the way out. It is fully in Norwegian. More home countries are coming next.
