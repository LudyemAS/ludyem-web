Why free calculators, from an app maker?

These tools give you the universal version of the math for free, in your browser, with nothing stored. They're the honest first pass. What they can't see is the part that actually decides your date: tax, pensions, and the account rules of where you live, which differ by country and can move a real retirement age by years.

That's the job of Runway, the app. It takes the same ideas, applies your country's real rules to your own figures, runs your plan through 400 market futures, and shows the levers that pull your date sooner, even retiring abroad. The calculators are the doorway; the app is the full plan. New reading meanwhile lives on the blog.
