Descriptions

One line

Runway is the FIRE planner built for Norway: it turns your money into one honest number, the age you could stop working.

Short, about 50 words

Runway is the first Financial Independence and Retire Early planner built for Norway. Answer a few questions and it gives you one honest number: the age your money could let you stop working. Then pull the levers that move it sooner. It models Norway's real tax rules, and everything stays on your iPhone.

Long

Every retirement calculator assumes American rules. Runway is built the other way around, on the rules that actually decide a Norwegian's retirement date. It models formuesskatt (wealth tax), the ASK account's capital-first withdrawals, the skjermingsfradrag, NAV and IPS pensions, and the utflyttingsskatt you would owe if you ever left the country.

The app starts simple, one motivating number from a few questions, then grows as deep as your finances do: a money-over-time chart, a range-of-futures Monte Carlo, a plan-health score, and an opportunity engine that shows exactly how many years each change buys you. It is fully localized in Norwegian, built natively in SwiftUI for iOS 26, and private by design: no account, no bank logins, nothing leaves your phone.

The story

Runway was built by an Italian who moved to Norway and went looking for a way to plan his future. Every retirement app he tried was built for Americans, blind to formuesskatt, to how an ASK account really works, to the exit tax he would owe if he ever moved back home. So he learned the Norwegian rules himself and turned them into the planner he wished he'd had when he arrived. Runway is the result: a serious financial tool that speaks Norwegian, respects your privacy, and gives you a straight answer to one question, when can I stop working.

What makes Runway different

  • Built for Norway, not adapted to it. The only FIRE planner that natively models formuesskatt, ASK and fondskonto, the skjermingsfradrag, NAV and IPS pensions, and utflyttingsskatt.
  • Adaptive depth. A few questions give a real freedom age in minutes; optional modules let people add only the parts of their financial life that apply, from a beginner's one number to a stress-tested plan.
  • Honest numbers. Progress is always real, never inflated. Figures carry a plain "estimate, not advice" footing, and the app never paywalls the answer to "when can I stop?"
  • Private by design. No account, no bank connections, no ads. Every projection is computed on the device.
  • Modern iOS. Home-screen and lock-screen widgets, a Siri shortcut for your freedom date, Swift Charts projections, iCloud sync, full VoiceOver and Dynamic Type, fully localized in Norwegian.
