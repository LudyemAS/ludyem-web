Frequently asked questions
Is {{APP_NAME}} free?+

{{APP_NAME}} is free to download and use. An optional Pro subscription unlocks advanced features — and includes a free trial so you can try it first.

Do I need an account?+

No. There's no account and no sign-up. Everything is stored on your device, and optionally synced across your own devices through your private iCloud. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why can't {{APP_NAME}} see my Apple Health data?+

The app needs your permission. Go to iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → {{APP_NAME}} and enable the categories you want. You can also grant permission again from the app's own settings.

How do I add a widget?+

Long-press an empty area of your home screen until the icons jiggle, tap the + in the corner, search for {{APP_NAME}}, choose a size, and tap Add Widget.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?+

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select {{APP_NAME}}, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro until the end of the current period. For refunds, use reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I restore my purchase on a new iPhone?+

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Pro, open {{APP_NAME}}, and go to Settings → Restore Purchases.
