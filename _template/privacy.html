Privacy Policy

Last updated {{DATE}} · Applies to {{APP_NAME}} by Ludyem (the "app").

We build {{APP_NAME}} so that your data stays on your device. We don't run servers that collect your personal information, we don't require an account, and we don't track you. This policy explains exactly what that means.

The short version

  • No account, no sign-up, no email required.
  • We don't collect, store, or sell your personal data.
  • Your data lives on your device (and in your own iCloud, if enabled).
  • No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Information we do not collect

The app has no backend that receives your data. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers for tracking, or your health and fitness data. Because there is no account system, there is nothing for us to log you into and nothing for us to store about you.

Health & fitness data

With your permission, {{APP_NAME}} integrates with Apple Health to read and/or write the data it needs to do its job. This data is managed by Apple Health on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy protections. The app never transmits your Health data off your device. You can review or revoke these permissions any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Data stored on your device

Your entries, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud, this data may sync across your own Apple devices via your private iCloud account, which is encrypted by Apple and not accessible to us.

Subscriptions & purchases

The app may offer an optional Pro subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store — we never see your payment details. Apple shares only anonymous, aggregated transaction information with us so we can confirm entitlements.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.
