Inside every app

A real trainer, not just a counter

The same engine powers all four apps — so whichever you pick, you get the full thing.

🎯

Adaptive plan

Tell it your current max and your goal — it builds a multi-week ladder of sets and rest that ramps you there at your chosen pace.

📷

Camera rep detection

For push-ups and sit-ups, on-device Vision counts your reps hands-free. Frames never leave your iPhone.

🔥

Streaks & records

Daily streaks you'll want to protect, plus personal-best tracking with a satisfying "new best" celebration.

⌚

Apple Watch trainer

A standalone watch app — count with taps or the Digital Crown, guided through your sets right on your wrist.

🏆

Game Center leaderboards

Compare your best session against everyone else training the same movement. Friendly, optional pressure.

🍎

Writes to Apple Health

Every set is logged as a workout in Apple Health, so your effort counts toward your rings and the rest of your data.

Privacy

On your iPhone. Full stop.

No account, no sign-in, no tracking. Camera rep detection runs entirely on-device — frames are analysed and discarded, never uploaded.
